Endian

How to arrange bit representation of object (more than one byte) in memory.

  • little endian: least significant bit come first (“first” means lower address).
    • Intel-compatible machines.
  • big ending: most significant come first.
    • IBM and Sun.

eg: How a int(4 bytes) stored in memory.

Boolean Algebra

c bitwise operators:

  • ^ xor
  • ~ not
  • & and
  • | or

Right shift

  • logical: fill left end with 0
  • arithmetic: fill left with most significant bit

Clever inplace_swap: use a^(b^a) == b

Cheatsheet for boolean algebra:

from Boolean algebra - Wikipedia

&

  • a & 0b0...0 == 0
  • a & 0b1...1 == a
  • a & a == a
  • distribution of and over or: x & (y | z) == (x & y) | (x & z)
  • distribution of or over and: x | (y & z) == (x | y) & (x | z)

|

  • a | 0b0...0 == a
  • a | 0b1...1 == 0b1...1
  • a | a == a
  • Absorption 1 and 2:
    • x & (x | y) == x
    • x | (x & y) == x

^

  • a ^ a == 0
  • a ^ (a ^ b) = b
  • If a and b are different, a ^ b != 0.

~

  • De Morgan’s law:
    • ~(x & y) == ~x & ~y
    • ~(x | y) == ~x | ~y

Deeper insight to De Morgan’s law: Duality Principle and group theory.

material implication: x->y

  • if x is true, take the y as value
  • else return true.

Unsigned and signed representation of integers

Signed number to its unsigned counterpart: T2U_w(x) == x + 2^w (x < 0).

unsigned to signed: U2T_w(x) == x - 2^w (x >= 2^(w-1)).

Floating point

Actually fractional binary numbers in scientific notation.

component (in case 1 Normalized Value):

  • sign s
  • significand M
    • fraction value f, where 0 <= f <= 1.
    • leading 1 representation: the significand M is defined as 1 + f.
  • exponent E
    • the exponent value is E(Unsigned number of this bit representation) + Bias
    • same as the two’s complement number’s highest 1.
    • Bias is 2^k - 1, to single is 127, double is 1023.

single: |s(1)|exp(8)|frac(23)|

double: |s(1)|exp(11)|frac(52)|

Case 2 Denormalized value.

Perpose: represent 0 and number close to 0.

When exponent field are all 0.

Value of representation:

  • Exponent value is E = 1 - Bias.
  • significand is M = f.

Case 3 Special Values

When fraction value are all ones.

  • Infinity: fraction field is all zeros.
    • +inf when s = 0.
  • NaN: not all zeros
    • short of “Not a Number”.

Rounding

  • Round to even(when no closer could be find).
    • won’t introduce a statistical bias.
  • Round towards zero
  • Round down
  • Round up

Floating point operations

Operation is Round(x*y).

It’s note associative. eg: (3.14 + 1e10) - 1e10 != 3.14
