English translation of Cui Jian's

Posted on 2020-02-28

This slower-tempo rock ballad, acts as an ironic social symbol in 1980s.

It’s one of my favourite piece. I do this translation as English training.

A Piece of Red Cloth

Cui Jian

Verse A1

That day, it was you that provide that red cloth,

which covered my eyes and my world.

You asked me what I see, I said I see the happiness.

Verse A2

With such a comfortable feeling,

I forgot that I don’t have a home.

You asked me where to go, I said I want to follow you.

Verse A3

Couldn’t see you, couldn’t see the road,

my hands were grabbed by you.

You asked me what I’m thinking, I said I would let you decide.

Chorus B1

I feel that you’re not the ion, but as strong and tough (as it).

I feel there are blood on you, for your hands are so hot and wet.

Chorus B2

I feel that here is not a wilderness, but I can’t see this land has become a drought.

I want to drink some water, but your lips have already covered my mouth.

Post Chorus

I couldn’t leave, neither could I wail, as my body has dried out.

I’m willing to accompany you this way, because I know most about your deepest pain.
